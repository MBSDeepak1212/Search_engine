 Chef wants to gift him a delicious cake. But Chef has spent most of his money in buying chocolate for himself and so he wants to get the best out of his remaining money. Now he has only M rupees remaining. He decides to make a delicious cake for his best friend with as much ingredients as possible. So Chef went to the super-market and found out that I ingredients are sold in each bag for a price of P rupees. There were N types of bags in the super-market and Chef can buy any number of bags of each type. He has to buy at least K number of ingredients to make his cake delicious, otherwise he will not be able to make a delicious cake. Your task is to help Chef make the most delicious cake for his friend.
