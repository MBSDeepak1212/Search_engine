 The new city contains exactly N houses. There are roads connecting the houses in such a way that there is exactly one way to go from a house to any other house. Length of each of the roads is known to Chef.Chief is doing a lot of research before shifting to the new city. He wants to know how many pairs of houses (a,b) are there, such that if we multiply length of all the roads on the path from a to b, the product will be divisible by M.
