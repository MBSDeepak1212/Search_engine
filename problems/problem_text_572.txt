You can choose one student and use an expert to train student and increase its level by 1. An expert can only be used once and for one student only. You can train the same student continuosly by using experts. But students of level N can't be trained because they hae maximum skills.Students upto (N-1) levels can be expertised. A student can maximally be trained upto Nth level. You are given number of students in each level and coding skill value of each level. Coding skill of your team is sum of skill value of all students. Find out the maximum possible coding skill of your team after using G experts.
