 The game is called Flippy Bird. Initially, Flippy will be standing on the number 0 on the number line. Then, Fluffy can perform n moves. For each move i, Fluffy can choose a direction, left or right. Then, Flippy will move in that direction for ai seconds. (and he travels 1 unit every second) Fluffy wins if Flippy's final position is between l and r inclusive. There are 2n possible ways to choose the directions for all n moves. Fluffy wants to know how many ways are there for him to win. Can you help him?\xc2\xa0
